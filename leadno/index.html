Client: American Express
Brand: American Express Gold Card
American Express wanted to sell their struggling Gold Card with $100 annual fee, in a market saturated by free Titanium & Platinum credit cards. They wanted a high visibility campaign on a shoe-string budget of $ 40,000 that could generate leads up to 40%, achieve 25% increase in sales, reach a threshold of 1000 acquisitions and make American Express an ‘accessible brand’.
While big banks chased HNIs, we changed the game. We tapped leaders of the future - Top B-School Graduates and pegged the Gold Card as a potent tool which would help them stay ahead of peers. We created a cost effective campaign on a platform they knew best - DIGITAL and gave them the clarion call to LEAD ON.
